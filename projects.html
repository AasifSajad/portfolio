HAOA — Hybrid Adaptive Optimization Algorithm

A research-style metaheuristic optimization framework combining adaptive learning, velocity-based movement, mutation refinement, elite exploitation, and Lévy-flight exploration — benchmarked head-to-head against Particle Swarm Optimization (PSO) across five standard nonlinear and multimodal test landscapes.

PythonOptimizationMetaheuristicsData Visualization
Problem SolvedComparing a custom hybrid optimizer against PSO to see which handles nonlinear, deceptive optimization landscapes better.
FeaturesBenchmark suite across Sphere, Rastrigin, Rosenbrock, Ackley & Griewank functions, with CSV result export and convergence plots.
ChallengesBalancing exploration vs. exploitation on rugged, deceptive landscapes without losing convergence stability.
What I LearnedNo single optimizer wins everywhere — HAOA beat PSO on Ackley and Rosenbrock, but PSO won on Rastrigin and Griewank.
